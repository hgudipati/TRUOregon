Our Premise

Rural voices carry
early warnings

Ashland's residents have lived through what Portland is beginning to face. Their experiences are data. Their adaptations are blueprints. This platform makes those connections visible.

Climate change doesn't arrive everywhere at once. The communities at the edge see it first — and they deserve to be heard.

Ashland sits at the climate frontier: longer fire seasons, declining snowpack, drought-stressed watersheds, and compounding emergencies. These aren't future projections — they're current reality for Rogue Valley residents.

Portland is tracking toward similar conditions within two decades. By creating a shared knowledge corridor between these communities, we can compress learning timelines and build more equitable responses to the climate challenges ahead.
